Defn. (Fundamental equations).

In general, a collateralization ratio is an unit amount of collateral over the unit amount of tokens.

CR=KT\text{CR} = \frac{K}{T} \\[0.5em] CR=TK​

In general, a priced collateralization ratio is the denominated amount of collateral over the settlement value of tokens at settlement rate (price) of ϕ\phiϕ collateral units per token units.

PCR=KTϕ\text{PCR} = \frac{K}{T\phi} \\[1em] PCR=TϕK​
